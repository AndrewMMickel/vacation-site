Camp Meriwether: The best place to camp near the beach

Available Activities

  • Camping (both classical sites and prebuilt structures available)
  • Easy access trails to and from beach
  • Archery ranges, blackpowder ranges, and more!

Availability

  • Open weekly for boyscouts
  • Most active during spring and summer
  • closed during winter
